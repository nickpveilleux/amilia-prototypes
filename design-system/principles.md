# Design Principles

Guiding principles for Amilia product design. When in doubt, these resolve ambiguity.

## Designer as Architect

The designer defines constraints, patterns, and rules. AI operates within them. The designer's job is not to author every screen — it's to design the system that generates correct screens.

## Consistency Over Novelty

Reuse existing patterns before inventing new ones. Amilia users manage complex operations — predictability reduces cognitive load.

## Clarity Over Density

When in doubt, give content more space. Use clear hierarchy (heading sizes, spacing, color) to guide the eye.

## Progressive Disclosure

Show what's needed, when it's needed. Don't overload a single view. Use cards, tabs, and expandable sections to organize complexity.

## Data First

Amilia is a data-heavy product. Tables, lists, and structured information should be easy to scan. Optimize for the user who's looking at hundreds of records, not the one looking at three.
